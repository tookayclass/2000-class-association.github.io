2021 National Championship venue : Torbay Yacht Club, Torquay
Monday, November 30, 2020

Its a fabulous venue for the Nationals. A boat park on the harbour wall. Launch from a concrete slipway into the marina. A short sail to the race area. Club a 5 minute walk from the boat park. Enjoy the RTYC ballroom, bar, dining room and garden all with fabulous Torbay views. An expert race organisation.
