The 2000 Class Association is run by keen volunteer 2000-dinghy sailors. The LDC 2000, formerly known as the Laser 2000, is an entry-level performance asymmetric sailing dinghy. It is equally suitable for cruisers, families as well as casual and serious racers.

The association organises a UK national circuit "Millennium Series", National Championships, Welsh Championships and Inlands Championships. It also delivers training sessions and provides support/advice for all.
